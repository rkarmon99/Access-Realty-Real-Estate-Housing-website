Looking For Sellers

Why Sell with an Access Realty real estate agent?

We will draw on our knowledge of the market to price your home to encourage qualified buyers to view your home, while making sure you “leave no money on the table.”

Pricing

Your real estate agent will draw on his or her knowledge of the market to make sure you “leave no money on the table,” but also that you don’t overprice your home so much that you discourage viewings by qualified buyers.

Showings

Your agent will supply a lockbox so that other Realtors can bring qualified buyers to see your house. A Realtor’s lockbox also tracks the comings and goings of all agents who have entered your home for the purposes of follow up and security.

Marketing

Your agent will market your house through the Multiple Listing Service website, agent networking, and other appropriate methods.

Buyers

Your agent will make sure prospective buyers are legitimate by assessment of the buyer’s ability to obtain a loan from trusted lenders.

Real Esate Contract

Your agent will bring market knowledge and problem-solving skills to the negotiation process and guide you through the contract-to-closing process, ensuring that deadlines are met, helping you make promised repairs by coordinating with reliable contractors, keeping you informed and accompanying you to the closing table.
